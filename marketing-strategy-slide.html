Marketing Strategy: Parents of Babies 0-24 Months

Objective: grow trust, trial, and repeat purchase by matching messages to the family stage and the practical need of each product line.

Strategic Direction

  • Life-stage targeting: one umbrella brand, three clear use-cases for the first 2 years.
  • Education-first communication: short practical guidance + soft emotional reassurance.
  • Confidence pathway: awareness -> first trial -> routine repurchase.

Primary Audience

Parents and caregivers of babies 0-24 months, with strongest focus on mothers in the first months and parents seeking reliable daily performance.

SEGMENT 1

Newborn (0-3 months)

Core need: gentle care in the first weeks.

  • Message: "Soft touch for delicate skin."
  • Proof points: soft materials, comfort-focused fit, easy frequent changes.
  • Content angle: first-weeks tips, sensitive-skin reassurance, pediatric-safe tone.
  • Best channels: maternity communities, parenting blogs, educational short videos.
SEGMENT 2

Active Babies (6-18 months)

Core need: secure fit while crawling and first steps.

  • Message: "Move freely, stay protected."
  • Proof points: reliable fixation, better fit in motion, less shifting.
  • Content angle: mobility moments, daytime play, parent convenience.
  • Best channels: social video, creator demos, e-commerce comparison blocks.
SEGMENT 3

Night Protection

Core need: uninterrupted sleep for baby and parents.

  • Message: "Dry nights, calmer mornings."
  • Proof points: higher absorbency, overnight confidence, fewer leak disruptions.
  • Content angle: bedtime routine, night-time peace of mind, family recovery.
  • Best channels: search intent pages, bedtime-focused creatives, CRM reminders.
Funnel KPI

CTR to product pages, add-to-cart rate, and trial conversion by segment.

Retention KPI

Repeat purchase rate in 30/60 days and cross-line progression as baby grows.

Brand KPI

Lift in trust and "best for my baby's needs" preference among parents.
